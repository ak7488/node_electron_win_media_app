n Next video (second screen).
u Volume up (second screen).
d Volume Down (second screen).

f 10 seconds forward (second screen).
b 10 second backword (second screen).

Ctrl + b decrease playback Speed by 0.5 (second screen).

Ctrl + a Maximise video screen. (global command)

Alt + k Close all screen.
Alt + c Close video screen.
Alt + a Close all screen.
Alt + v Toggle video opeanear.
Alt + m + a Maximise main screen. (global command)
